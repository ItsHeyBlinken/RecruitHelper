# Texas College Softball Athletic Websites (Updated)

This document contains a comprehensive list of athletic websites for colleges in Texas with softball programs, categorized by division. These URLs can be used for scraping coach and recruiter contact information.

### Verification Notes:
- **Removed (no varsity NJCAA softball):** Southwest Texas Junior College, Texarkana College, Laredo College.
- **TCU & Rice:** Both schools currently only offer softball at the **Club** level and do not have NCAA varsity programs.
- **Dallas College:** Softball programs are specifically hosted at the **Brookhaven, Eastfield, North Lake, and Richland** campuses.
- **UT Permian Basin:** Correct athletics site is **utpbfalcons.com** (not utpbbasinguns.com).
